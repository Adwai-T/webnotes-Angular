      <h3>Assumptions for Techincal Analysis</h3>
      <ol type="A">
        <li>
          <strong>Market Discounts Everything.</strong>
          The how of the trends is more important that the why of market action.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Price Moves in Trends.</strong>
          The Trends in price are a fundamental foundation on which Technical Analysis is based on.
          Once such trends are established the price moves in the same direction until the trends are reversed.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>History Tends to Repeat itself.</strong>
          The market trends in similar situations tend to repeat themselves. That means studing past trends can gives us
          information about how the market might behave in the future.
        </li>
      </ol>
